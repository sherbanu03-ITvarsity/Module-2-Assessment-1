THE INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is home to one of the largest and most diverse insect collections in the world. Here, you will see a variety of insects with unique characteristics, physical features and their accustomed habitats. We try to source these insects from all over the world just for you to be exposed to an optimal learning journey.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

The Insect House can be found in the Eastern part of the zoo, directly opposite the Monkey Trail.
